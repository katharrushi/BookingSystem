Play's in Theater

Old Man and Old Moon is a whimsical theatrical journey of an old man who embarks on a mission to find his missing moon, encountering adventures and love along the way.

The Bose Legacy is a play that explores the life and legacy of Indian freedom fighter Subhas Chandra Bose, delving into his ideologies, struggles, and sacrifices in the fight for independence.

Macbeth is a tragedy by Shakespeare that explores ambition, power, and the consequences of unchecked desire as the titular character becomes consumed by guilt and paranoia after murdering the king.

The Merchant of Venice is a play by Shakespeare that explores themes of justice, mercy, and prejudice as a moneylender seeks to collect a pound of flesh from a merchant.

Natasamrat is a play in Marathi that portrays the tragic downfall of a veteran actor, exploring themes of family, relationships, and the fleeting nature of fame and success.
